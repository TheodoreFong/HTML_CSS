Taking Chances

Posted on February 04, 2013

Our biggest fear is being rejected. Yet sometimes, our fears prevent us from reaching to greater things. As people we constantly think about what other people think about us. That is our biggest weapon, but also our biggest vulnerability. It is important to gain confidence in speaking what is on your mind and seizing the day.
