Bucket List

In the true spirit of living, we need to make the most out of our lives. I found that by creating some lifetime goals, I was able to find more direction within my life. It is useful to ask yourself “What do you want to do before you die?” Take some time alone and put it out on paper to draw out your real thoughts. They don’t have to be things that you might be able to do right now. Dream big! Finally, whether it is complete or not, share it! A bucket list is a great way to get to know other people and discover new things!

This is my bucket list and although it changes whenever I discover new things, it also helps me “Seize the Day!” and truly live to learn as I learn how to live.
